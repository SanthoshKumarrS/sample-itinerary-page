Cherai Beach

Golden Sand Calm Waters Beach Activities

Cherai Beach, stretching over 15 kilometers, is known for its golden sand, palm groves, and seashells. Perfect for swimming and sunbathing, the beach offers a unique blend of sea and backwaters. Visitors can enjoy various activities including dolphin watching, boating, and beach games.

Fort Kochi Beach

Historical Site Chinese Fishing Nets Sunset Views

Fort Kochi Beach is famous for its historic Chinese fishing nets and colonial architecture. The beach provides a perfect vantage point for watching spectacular sunsets and local fishermen at work. The area is also known for its seafood restaurants and cultural performances.

Kuzhupilly Beach

Serene Environment Natural Beauty Less Crowded

Kuzhupilly Beach is a hidden gem known for its pristine waters and peaceful environment. Less crowded than other beaches, it's perfect for those seeking solitude and natural beauty. The beach is surrounded by coconut groves and offers excellent opportunities for photography and meditation.
