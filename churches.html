Santa Cruz Basilica

One of the oldest churches built by European colonists in India, Santa Cruz Basilica is a historic church that showcases Indo-European and Gothic architecture. Its interior features beautiful frescos, paintings, and ornate decorations.

Timings: 7:00 AM - 6:30 PM
Photography Allowed
Built in 1505, rebuilt in 1905

St. Francis Church

The oldest European church in India, St. Francis Church is known for its historical significance. Vasco da Gama was originally buried here in 1524. The church features simple architecture with a gabled timber-framed roof.

Timings: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM
Protected Monument
Originally built in 1503

Vallarpadam Church

The Basilica of Our Lady of Ransom is a major pilgrimage center. Known for its beautiful location on Vallarpadam Island, this church is dedicated to Mary, known locally as Vallarpadathamma.

Timings: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Feast: September 24
Established in 1524
