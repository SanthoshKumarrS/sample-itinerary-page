Ernakulam Shiva Temple

One of the major temples in Kochi, the Ernakulam Shiva Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is known for its traditional Kerala architecture and beautiful murals. The annual festival here is a grand celebration that attracts devotees from all over.

Timings: 4:30 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Best Time: During Shivaratri

Chottanikkara Bhagavathy Temple

This ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathy. Known for its powerful spiritual energy, the temple attracts devotees seeking blessings and healing. The architecture showcases classic Kerala temple style with intricate wooden carvings.

Timings: 4:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Best Time: During Navaratri

Thirumala Devaswom Temple

Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, this temple is built in the traditional South Indian style. The temple is known for its peaceful atmosphere and regular cultural programs. The annual festival features traditional temple arts and music.

Timings: 5:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Best Time: During Brahmotsavam
